Created in France and then developed in the atelier of an old Japanese soy sauce brewing company (est.1882), the Oiseau Merveilleux products bring the best from both worlds. Which offers a unique and timeless addition to food solutions. Hence, in 2022, Oiseau Merveilleux has decided to make this delicacy readily available to the world, by sharing the magic of its sauce.

Our extensive research led us to the revelation that there is no Asian sauce that is linked to the words luxury and gourmet. Oiseau Merveilleux, as a brand, is a fusion of Japanese, Indonesian and Chinese traditions, culture, ingredients and savoir-faire with a hint of the exquisite gourmet food of France. Our wonder sauces, which are brewed through traditional soybean fermentation, are unique recipes that revamp the cooking experience for all types of cooks, ranging from amateurs to professionals. We provide versatile sauces with refined Asian flavors offering an unprecedented culinary experience through its luxury glass packaging and designs, which can also be upcycled.
